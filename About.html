About Me

Since my first website in Elementary school, I've always been intrigued by the designs and capabilities of software development. Jump ahead 8 years, I became a problem solver, project manager, and maintenance planner in the United States Navy. From leading up to 50 other sailors in ship-wide tasks to personally mentoring individuals, I've cultivated a wide range of personal and professional skills. These skills help me not only work with peers and lead superiors, but also maintain and create mutually beneficial relationships between the Navy and civilian contractors.

After years of working with my hands and taking apart, troubleshooting, and repairing a variety of machines on Naval vessels to UPS, Fedex, and Amazon distribution centers all over Southern California, I have finally returned to the tech field. Software development has always been my calling, and now I'm back. I've gained familiarity with a variety of programming languages and platforms through bootcamps and self-teaching. I'm eager to continue expanding my knowledge and honing my current skills, and to meet other software engineers, feel free to connect!
