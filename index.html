Dr. Victor Krumm was once a brilliant scientist and philanthropist, but after a series of events beyond his control, he became one of the world's most feared supervillains. Born in a small Eastern European town, Victor showed a talent for science from a young age and quickly rose through the ranks of the academic community. However, his life changed forever when his wife and children were killed in a freak accident caused by one of his experiments gone wrong. Fueled by anger and a desire for revenge, Victor turned his talents towards developing advanced technologies with the goal of dominating the world. He has since become the mastermind behind numerous world-threatening events, using his vast wealth and scientific knowledge to create powerful weapons and hire a team of deadly henchmen.
